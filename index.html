Who We Are?

Foresight Consulting and Services (FCS) is a limited liability private firm established to provide learning and development support to professionals, networks, organizations and institutions to facilitate the attainment of their strategic objectives.

What We Do?

We offer innovative, market-driven and time tested solutions to your learning and development challenges through advisory, capacity development and management support. Our approach hinges on participatory processes enabling partners to contribute and to own solutions thus achieving sustainability.

How We do it?

Our approach to jobs is based on a three-phase framework including fact-finding, diagnosis and change. This enables our consultants to understand and appreciate the client’s real or perceived challenges from a 360⁰ view and thereby prescribing solutions that are real and in sync with circumstances before the client’s business context and culture. We constantly ensure that we apply a suite of widely recognized and professionally accepted management systems that are both robust and reliable.

Training

Our programs

Management Trainee Program(MTP)

The program is targeted at the youth especially those at the entry level on the management ladder. The aim is to equip them with practical yet real management and leadership skills, techniques and tools to enable them discharge their management duties efficiently and effectively. It is a yearlong relationship with the trainee in which we provide coaching and mentoring to support the process.

On-demand Training(ODT)

We take inventory of your personal or organizational situation in terms of capacity deficits and design appropriate capacity response.

Open-Subscription Training(OST)

We offer generic courses tailored to no specific client. Anybody who wants to upgrade their skills and knowledge about a particular subject is free to subscribe and attend. Participants receive certificate of participation after successful completion of the course.
